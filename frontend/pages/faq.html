Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using the Warehouse Management System.

🔍
What is the Warehouse Management System (WMS)? +
WMS is a full-stack platform for managing warehouse operations — inventory, zones, racks, shelves, stock movements, approvals, and analytics — all from one dashboard, with role-based access for different teams.
Do I need to install anything to use WMS? +
No installation is required. WMS runs entirely in your browser — just sign in with your account and you're ready to go on desktop or mobile.
How do I create an account? +
Click "Get Started" or "Create one" on the login page, fill in your name, username, email, and password, choose a role, and submit. You'll be redirected to sign in once your account is created.
I forgot my password. What should I do? +
Contact your warehouse administrator to have your password reset. Admins can reset credentials for any user from the Users management screen.
What are the different user roles in WMS? +
WMS supports seven roles: Admin, Manager, Supervisor, Staff, Warehouse Staff, Viewer, each with different levels of access — from full system control to read-only dashboards.
How do I add or update inventory items? +
Go to the Inventory page from your dashboard sidebar, then use "Add Item" to create a new product or click an existing item to edit quantities, location, and details — subject to your role's permissions.
How are warehouses, zones, racks, and shelves organized? +
WMS uses a hierarchy: a Warehouse contains Zones, each Zone contains Racks, and each Rack contains Shelves. This structure lets you track exact storage locations and capacity at every level.
How do stock movement approvals work? +
Inventory adjustments and transfers submitted by Staff are routed to the Approvals queue, where Managers or Supervisors can approve or reject them before the stock levels are finalized.
Can I export reports? +
Yes. The Reports page lets you export inventory, stock movement, and analytics data as PDF, CSV, or Excel files for offline review or sharing.
Does WMS support demand forecasting? +
Yes — WMS includes an AI-powered demand forecasting module that uses historical stock movement data to predict future demand and suggest restock quantities per product.
Is my data secure? +
WMS uses JWT-based authentication, role-based access control, and encrypted connections to protect your data. Only authorized users can access information relevant to their role.
Where can I review the Terms & Conditions? +
You can read the full Terms & Conditions any time on our Terms & Conditions page. You'll also be asked to review and accept them before signing in.
