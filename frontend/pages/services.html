⚙️ Platform Capabilities

Everything You Need
to Run a Warehouse

Six core modules covering every stage of your warehouse lifecycle — from raw stock intake to AI-powered demand forecasting.

📦
Live Stock Tracking
Module 01

Inventory Management

Track stock levels in real time across all warehouses with automated low-stock alerts and reorder triggers built in.

  • Real-time quantity tracking per product
  • Low-stock threshold alerts
  • Category-based organisation
  • Multi-warehouse support
🏢
Zone → Rack → Shelf
Module 02

Warehouse Structure

Organise your physical space into a hierarchy of warehouses, zones, racks, and shelves — each with capacity tracking.

  • Hierarchical zone / rack / shelf model
  • Capacity percentage monitoring
  • Visual occupancy indicators
  • Shelf-level product assignment
🔄
Full Audit Trail
Module 03

Stock Movements

Log every inbound, outbound, and internal transfer movement with a complete, tamper-proof audit history.

  • Inbound / outbound / transfer types
  • Timestamped movement log
  • User-attributed actions
  • Filter and export movement history
📈
Charts & Exports
Module 04

Analytics & Reports

Visual dashboards and one-click exportable reports covering occupancy rates, stock value, and movement trends.

  • Interactive occupancy charts
  • PDF, CSV & Excel exports
  • Stock value summaries
  • Movement trend graphs
✅
Role-gated Approvals
Module 05

Approvals Workflow

Managers can approve or reject inventory adjustments and stock transfer requests with full role-based access controls.

  • Pending / approved / rejected states
  • Manager & admin approval gates
  • Notification on status change
  • Complete approval audit log
🤖
Linear Regression AI
Module 06

AI Demand Forecasting

Linear regression-powered demand predictions analyse historical movement data to help you stock smarter and cut waste.

  • scikit-learn regression model
  • Per-product demand predictions
  • Restock quantity recommendations
  • Trend-based alerts
